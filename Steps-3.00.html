Step 3 - Publish/ Upload 3

Starting with PowerBI Desktop, 

Sign into the PBI Service by using the credentials below.






Click on the Publish Icon and choose the Power BI Production Workspace



Go to PowerBI.com and login with the same credentials as before
 

Go to Workspaces and choose the Power BI Alumni Workspace. 




After you have reviewed the report, click on the Update App Icon in the top right corner. 



Click on Update App in the  bottom right hand corner and then Go to App


Now the End User can see the report through the App
