Step 4 - Unpublish/ remove 4

How to Unpublish

At any time and under any circumstances Admin can remove published Power BI reports from Colaberry Power BI Cloud.  

- Go to the right corner of the window and click on the ellipse... get the options 

- make a selection to the Unpublish

- make sure that the will remove the report completely from the viewers
