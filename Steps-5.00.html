Sharing your Dashboard Externally 5
  1. How to embed Power BI reports into portals
  2. Select Share > Embed report > Publish to web (public)


3. Accept the Warnings

4. Click the HTML copy button

FYI - When you do your Stress Test and you are posting the HTML link, change the width and height to 100% after copying the link.
